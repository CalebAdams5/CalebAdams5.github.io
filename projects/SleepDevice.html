Smart Sleep Device

Overview

Designed and developed a sleep-enhancing device using CAD modeling, MATLAB, and Arduino. The project focused on creating a functional prototype through iterative design and testing.

Design and Prototyping

Created CAD models and developed multiple prototypes to evaluate the device's functionality, size, and usability. Design iterations were completed based on user feedback, reducing the overall device size by 40%.

Testing and Validation

Integrated electronic components and validated system functionality through prototype testing. MATLAB and Arduino were used to support system development and evaluation.

Results

Presented the completed design at the Ohio State University Engineering Design Competition, where the project ranked in the top 20% of over 200 teams.
