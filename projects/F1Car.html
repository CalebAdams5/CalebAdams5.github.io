3D Printed F1 Car

Overview

Designed and built a battery-powered Formula 1 style model car using CAD, 3D printing, and electrical components. The project combined mechanical design, rapid prototyping, and electronics to create a functional vehicle.

Design Process

Developed the vehicle using Fusion 360 CAD software and produced the components with 3D printing. The design evolved through multiple iterations to improve performance, assembly, and overall functionality.

Assembly

Integrated motors, electrical wiring, a 9V battery, and a fan propulsion system into the printed chassis. Soldering techniques were used to create reliable electrical connections between components.

Testing and Improvements

Built an initial cardboard prototype before manufacturing the final design. Testing identified opportunities for improvement, leading to several design revisions before the completed vehicle was assembled.
